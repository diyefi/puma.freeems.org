Welcome to the Puma board page!

 

Puma is an automotive-grade board designed to run FreeEMS vanilla code, an Open Source Engine Management System destined to liberate engines all around the globe. This piece of hardware was meant to meet the highest reliability standards, and to provide most of the functions needed by engine tuners. It has the potential to sequentially drive up to 8 cylinder engines, controlling both fuel and ignition (wasted spark), or dual rail 4 cylinders setups, 6cyl, etc. With the appropiate firmware, almost anything can be done.

These boards come with military temperature specs, and plenty of heavy duty components.
