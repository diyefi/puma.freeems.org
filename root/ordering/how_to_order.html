Ordering information

 

Right now, we're still testing the hardware, the first prototypes are being assembled and installed by a small group of qualified people around the world.

Once we have a tested system, boards can be ordered in this site, thorugh the bulk buy forum section, or by contacting me.


If you really want a board, mail me to let us know about your interest. Once the next release is ready for production you'll be notified and invited to register in the forum to coordinate the manufacture.
